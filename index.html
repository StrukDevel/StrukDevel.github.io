My Services

Below are the different types of photography I offer. If you're interested in something

that isn't listed, don't hesitate to contact me!

Mouse over any of the below images to read a bit about that type of photography.

Family Portraits

Shooting Family Portraits is one of my favourite sessions. It's a must-have if you're looking to take full advantage of my experience and professionalism. I am to capture the emotion and love of each family.

Portrait Session

Client's understand that this type of photography is a neccessity for those starting out in the professional world. A professional portrait will make a great first impression. Also for those that would like to capture their own expressions.

Engagement

I understand that this is the first cost towards your soon to be special day. Allow me to capture the first few moments of engagement so you can share amazing photographs with the amazing people in your life, and be able to cherish these moments forever.

Wedding

Many clients approach me with requests to capture this special moment of their lives. I understand how much you have going on at this time, which is why it's my goal to make this day easier for you. Let me capture your special day, and I promise it will be hassle-free with priceless photos.

Pets

Don't forget your furry little friends! Doing photos of other's fur babies is a speciality of my Photography. I aim to capture the personality of each client's animal to the best of my ability.

Automotive

An area I have recently ventured into. I have grown passionate for taking breathtaking photos of your car/bike. Allow me to take photos of your vehicle like none other, so you can show of your prized possesion even more.
